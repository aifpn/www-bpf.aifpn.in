What is BPF:

BPF i.e. Bharat Protection Force, which is responsible for the security of All India Food Processing Nigam. All India Food Processing Nigam recruit male and female female constable for Bharat Protection Force by conducting BPF constable exam.

What is the eligibility to join BPF:

  1. 1. To apply in BPF, it is necessary to pass at least 10th standard.
  2. 2. To apply in BPF, your age limit should be between 18 to 25 years. Apart from this, there is a relaxation in the upper age limit of 5 years for SC / ST candidates and 3 years for OBC candidates.
  3. 3. BPF also has physical measurement. In which the height of the male should be from 160 cm to 165 cm. At the same time, the height of the woman should be from 152 cm to 157 cm.
  4. 4. To take admission in BPF, the minimum chest measurement of a male should be 80 cms. At the same time, it should increase by 5 cm on inflating.   5. 5. Before taking admission in BPF, candidates are made to run, long jump, high jump for physical efficiency test. In which the men's 1600 meters run is of 5 minutes 45 seconds. And there is a woman's 800 meters. Which has to be completed in 3 minutes 40 seconds. Men's long jump is 14 feet and women's is 9 feet. Men's high jump is up to 4 feet and women's is up to 3 feet.
  6. 6. Medical examination is also done in BPF. In which the eyes of the candidates should be 6/6. And your eyes should have the power to recognize colors. That is, there should not be a problem of color build-up.
  7. 7. You should not be one of the persons wearing glasses due to eye problem to be admitted in BPF. Also hearing should not be a problem.
  8. 8. In this, your weight should be right according to your age.
  9. 9. You must be a citizen of India to join BPF.
